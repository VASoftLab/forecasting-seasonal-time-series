When you submit your paper to our journal:

(1) You will need to prepare your manuscript using our journal's
template (see the Instructions for Authors:
https://www.mdpi.com/journal/algorithms/instructions). Moreover, you
could submit a cover letter including the simple introduction of your
paper and indicating you are invited by Stella Xu, so that I can check
your submission and apply for the discount;
(2) Please register (http://susy.mdpi.com/user/register/) and log in
(http://susy.mdpi.com/user/login/) to the system before your submission.
Please check junk or spam mail box if you have not received the active
link of your account;
(3) After registration, please access the link
(https://susy.mdpi.com/user/manuscripts/upload), and enter your account,
click "Submit Manuscript" under Submissions Menu;
(4) Choose "Yes" for the option "Used template?" and upload your source
file (Word/Latex Zip);
(5) Fill in manuscript details from Steps 1 to 5:
Journal: Algorithms
Research Topics:
(6) Click the "submit" button after you finish all the steps. An
auto-generated email will send to your mailbox to inform you that your
submission is successful.

Please tell us your manuscript ID after you submit it successfully, so
that we can check your submission.
